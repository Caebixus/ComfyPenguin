How to measure sweatshirt

Sweatshirts tend to be such type of fabric which need ample care as far as measurement and sizing is concerned. In the same way, followings are a few tips and methods which could be ideally helpful.

A = Length / Front Length

Simply place the measuring tape at the middle of jumper and start measuring it from back side of collar seam to all the way down to desired hemline.

B = Width / Chest / Waist

For chest, try to measure around the fullest part by keeping the tape closer to aspired hemline of the body.
