Finding Comfy clothes Online Isn't Easy—Here’s How




Online shopping can be tough. There’s no way to try on items before you buy them, so there’s a chance of ending up with a bunch of ill-fitting clothing that wasn’t at all what you thought you ordered.

Though most online stores offer sizing options and guides, sizes can vary widely from platform to platform. A size 10 dress from one store could be smaller than a size 8 dress from another, causing stress and confusion for the buyer.

Since sizing varies so widely in how clothing is traditionally measured, finding clothing online is a daunting task.


And when you’re looking for comfy clothes, the last thing you want is an item that doesn’t fit right. There’s a lot of trial and error when shopping for clothing, because not all bodies are the same.

Clothes fit everyone differently, depending on their shape. Maybe you have broad shoulders or short legs. That will affect the way clothes fit you, distinctions you can’t predict based on the overly-broad categorization of “size 4” or “medium.” A better, more personalized sizing option is needed.
