- Our Mission -

To provide new fashion based on the sizes of your already used and favorite clothing

My name is David. And I develop quite a unique website called Comfy-penguin.com

The idea for this project was born in 2014 when I noticed that there were no straightforward websites for search by clothing parameters. Just e-commerce sites, with clothing I was interested, but not sure if the size is right. My idea was simple: measure your favorite sweatshirt and find the same-sized sweatshirts according to these parameters.

I believe this is very effective and if the parameters from size charts are precise, this method could bring time-saving. Also, if we could discover which parameters are heavily used and which are not, it could lead us to a more sustainable fashion world.

The fashion industry could focus on clothing parameters which are mostly used and apply more sustainable materials in order to protect our environment for the next generations!
