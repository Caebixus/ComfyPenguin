What is dress size calculator and why are so many people using it?



Shopping for clothes online is quite awkward for many, and you have the feeling that you are buying something that you don't want in reality.

There is still a problem with the size of the clothes. Because we are different beings with different bodies, it creates a huge problem when shopping online for clothes.

So there may be clothes that you absolutely love, but they don't fit you when they arrive, and eventually, you will have to return them. How does it work?

Our dress size calculator works by measuring your favorite clothes (currently only jumpers, sweatshirts and hoodies) according to the most widely used parameters, that is, width and length of a product.

Why use a dress size calculator?

It works great if you want to buy a new sweater or sweatshirt for your loved ones, but don't know their size. And of course, you don't want to ask them about their size even if they knew it from their heads and also because it was supposed to be a surprise.

It's easy, first, you have to observe what kind of clothes the person wears the most. Once you know which to measure it. In the case of a sweater or T-shirt, it is by default the width and length.
