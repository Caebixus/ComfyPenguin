Hoodie size chart compare to size search engine?



The hooded sweatshirt also called “hoodie” is a very popular part of everyone’s wardrobe and the trend of wearing hoodies is unstoppable even in 2019, that is why we have a choice of endless sheets of different tips, colors, and shapes for these hoodies. The hoodie is a typical streetwear fashion, and when we look at when it is worn by men, it will cut us off for almost every occasion.

Whether it's a hangover at home, a park run or a party with friends. Here everywhere hoodie holds us.

In this article, I want to talk about the hoodie size chart. Because of thousands of hoodies and different brand, there are different sizes and measurements.

You can never rely on your chosen size, which works for one sweatshirt that you wear happily for years, such as the size of S, you will fall in another shop or manufacturer.


So what should we do?

First of all, you need to get parameters of your best, most comfortable hoodie you have.

If you want to buy hoodie for someone, you should observe which hoodie that person wear the most. Then you need to get basic parameters like width and length of a hoodie.


Pictures below show my best hoodie that I love even today and how to measure it with precision. Be careful, the measuring tape should be taught.


With my measuring tape, I know that length is 72cm and width is around 53cm.


If I copy these parameters to my size search engine it shows that there are more than 100+ Tops for men with these parameters.

Although it shows all products with +2cm from my measuring parameters, it is a good sign.

Now if you could look closer on sizes with different brands you can see that I prove my point.

Different brands have indeed different sizes and we, as a customer, are not able to see it at first look easily.

This finding really pissed me off and also motivated me to continue with comfy-penguin.com with its size search engine.


What I want to do is to create an online tool to help search and find clothes with exactly the same parameters as your best, most comfortable clothes you wear daily.

With this data, you could find a new one without fearing that you end up with a completely wrong size.
