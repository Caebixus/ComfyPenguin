How to measure sweatshirt

To determine the exact size and fit for you, you simply need to measure a few precise points of a pullover or sweater you already own and then compare the measurements with our size chart.

To be sure you are measuring with accuracy, we advise you to first lay the garment on a table or any other flat surface.

Step One. Get measurements of your favourite clothing!


Choose your favourite, most comfy clothing you want to buy and get parameters (for example width and length). You can use smartphone measure apps to get your sizes, but I recommend to measure it with measuring tape or meter.
